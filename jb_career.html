Career

Introduction

Jimmy Buffett began his music career in Nashville in the late 1960's. He recorded his first album in 1970. Buffett moved to Key West in 1971 and subsequently started establishing the easy-going beach bum persona for which he is primarily known. He has recorded 29 albums, is known as a popular concert draw, and continues to tour throughout the year. He has written soundtracks and made several cameo appearances in movies and television. Buffett has also launched several business ventures, including the tropical-themed Margaritaville chain of restaurants.
